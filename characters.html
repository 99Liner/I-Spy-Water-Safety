Game Characters

Bear Rangers

Description

Every bear ranger is uniquely dressed in a vibrant outfit consisting of a cross-body bag, matching slacks, a shirt with paw prints, and a cap with an insignia. The color designs range from blue and green to yellow, and every bear embodies the friendly guardian of the wild, seeming cheerful and welcoming.

Chick Rangers

Description

Each chick ranger is distinctively dressed in a vivid uniform that includes matching shorts, a utility belt with essential ranger tools, and a cap featuring the park insignia. These animated characters, with their youthful and bright appearances, are designed to appear as genial and inviting guardians of the wilderness.

Deer Rangers

Description

Each deer ranger wears a badge-adorned collared shirt and a utility belt as part of a well-tailored uniform. Their clothes, in refreshing tones of blue and aqua, fit their calm, collected role as forest guardians. These figures serve as protectors of the forest, radiating a sense of friendliness and calm assurance.

Wolf Rangers

Description

The wolf ranger has casual yet official outfit that includes a green checked shirt, paired with black trousers. Its wolfish features, like the pointed ears and grey fur, are rendered in a soft and approachable manner, aligning with its role as a welcoming figure and protector of the digital forest setting.
